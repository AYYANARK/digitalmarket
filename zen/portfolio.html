Condos le M Lorraine

This logo has a completely contemporary look but will still delight nostalgic baby boomers with its retro mosaic color schemes reminiscent of popular patchworks from the 70s.

An M within an M which illustrates the concept of "inhabiting one's space", the content in the container, the person in their world, in their own condo. In addition, this duplication of the M also refers to the fact that the M Lorraine project is fully endorsed by the Magma Group, whose logo is also constructed from triangles and diagonals.
